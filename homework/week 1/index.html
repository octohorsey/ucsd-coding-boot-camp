About Me

Hufflepuff or Ravenclaw? Lately, that has been my preoccupation-- that, somehow, my placement in the ficticious Hogwarts house matter.

I grew up in L.A. and lived in San Diego for the past five or so years-- but in between, I've worn many hats.

-- I attended UCLA for a degree in Molecular Biology. After finishing school, I searched for a niche and gravitated toward the engineering. I went back to school, to Cal Poly Pomona, for a masters in Electrical Engineering, and worked for the past five years at a San Diego-area Department of Defense research lab.

And now, I've set upon my next great adventure-- into the thicket of Software Engineering.

It was by the end of Harry Potter that we learned that the Houses never really mattered-- they were superficial veneer for the stuff of courage, conviction, and sacrifice. I'm still finding my profession, but wherever I end up, I'm eager to dwell in creativity, fly in teamwork, and grow to great technical achievement.
